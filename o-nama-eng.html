About us

Who are we?


At ISKON, we understand that efficient transportation is essential for the success of any business. That's why we've spent over 20 years perfecting our logistics services to ensure that our clients can focus on what they do best - running their businesses.

Our family-owned business is built on a long tradition of integrity and a commitment to putting our clients first.

What services do we provide?


Our comprehensive services include transport organization, LTL, FTL, maritime transport, air transport, freight forwarding, and warehousing.

With a vast network of partners and a flexible approach to business, we provide fast and efficient solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

Our core values?


We have managed to develop long-term partnerships based on a set of core values that determine everything we do:

1.Excellence: We are committed to providing the highest level of service and support to our clients, while also continuously improving our internal systems and processes.

2.Collaboration: We believe that collaboration is key to success, which is why we work closely with our clients, partners, and suppliers to achieve common goals.

3.Innovation: We are always looking for innovative and efficient ways to improve, whether it's through technology, processes, or partnerships.

Why should you work with us?


As a leader in the market in groupage transport organization throughout Europe, ISKON has the expertise and resources to optimize your transportation needs and provide you with peace of mind.

Let us take care of your logistics, so you can focus on growing your business.
