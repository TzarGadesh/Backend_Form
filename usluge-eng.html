Our services

FTL(Full truck load)

We understand the importance of efficient and reliable transportation for your business. That's why we offer full-load import and export services on a regular basis to ensure your goods are delivered on time. Our road transport services include FTL (Full Truck Load) that can be carried out using any type of vehicle, ensuring flexibility and customization to meet your specific needs. We also offer specialized vehicles for oversized cargo transport, ADR cargo transport, and temperature-controlled cargo transport to ensure that every load is transported with care every step of the way. Our team has the knowledge and resources to handle any challenge that may arise, guaranteeing that even the most complex requests will be answered to your utmost satisfaction.

LTL (Less than truck load)

Our team specializes in organizing partial(groupage) transport throughout Europe, Turkey and Russia. We provide transportation services for a wide range of goods, using all types of vehicles, in order to meet the needs of our clients. Thanks to our extensive network of partners, we can respond quickly to inquiries and provide tailored solutions for each client. Our partial transport service allows you to combine smaller shipments and transport them together, which can be very cost-effective solution for businesses of all sizes. With us, you can rest assured that your goods will arrive at their destination safely and in the shortest possible time.

Sea freight

We offer worldwide shipping services for cargo transport. Whether it's a small amount of goods or a large shipment, we organize transportation from the port to the desired address. Our goal is to provide you with a simple and reliable way of transporting your goods. To make the transportation process as easy as possible, we offer two types of loading: Less than Container Load (LCL) and Full Container Load (FCL). Regardless of the type of goods you are transporting, we can provide you with an appropriate container for transportation.

Air freight

If you need a quick and efficient solution for delivering your shipment due to a deadline, we offer air freight services. We provide delivery to all major airports worldwide. For an import of goods into Serbia in addition to air transport to Belgrade Airport, we can also provide customs clearance and delivery to the final destination.

Storage of goods

We can offer you storage of goods through network of our partners with customs warehouses at all customs offices in Belgrade. Our partners have high-quality facilities for storing goods, equipped with modern inventory tracking and management systems, as well as fire and theft protection. Our team will provide you with the best possible solution tailored to your needs, regardless of the type or quantity of goods. We can offer you both short-term and long-term storage of goods at our warehouses.

Freight forwarding

In addition to our transport and warehousing services, we offer freight forwarding services that include customs clearance for your exports and imports. Our team of customs procedure experts will assist you in ensuring that your goods are properly cleared and transported to their destination in accordance with regulations. We can also advise you on the necessary documentation for customs procedures and re-export. Our freight forwarders will be available to you throughout the process to ensure that your goods are safe and sound.
